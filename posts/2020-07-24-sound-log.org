At [[https://www.recurse.com/][recurse center]], most textual communication happens in a private zulip instance for members of the community there. Within, there is a ~#music~  stream where people can share what they are listening too. This is sometimes themed, sometimes mixed. I started a topic there called "sound log" where I just put whatever I found that day, and many of my go-tos for getting in the right space.
